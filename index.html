Royal Decree

Let it be known across the realm,
that Sayantani holds the entirety of my heart.
You are the absolute center of my world,
and I vow to cherish you endlessly.
To my beautiful queen, today and always:
Your happiness is my greatest priority.
❤️
Look inside my heart...
You are the dawn that breaks my darkest night,
The ruling sovereign of my guiding light.
Every beat of my heart belongs to you,
A quiet promise, absolute and true.
No kingdom holds a treasure quite so rare,
No crown can match the beauty that you wear.
I look at you and finally understand,
That my whole world is resting in your hand.
Command my days, and I will gladly yield,
My love your armor, and my soul your shield.
Forever yours, my queen, my chosen throne,
I belong to you, and you alone.
